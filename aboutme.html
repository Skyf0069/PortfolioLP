Hi there !

My name is Lucille, but you can also call me Lushifer,

I am a french artist whos' passionate about cinema, music and writing stories. I'm currently in 1st year of DN MADe animation in valence.

About me

I inspire myself from a large variety of films, musics and artists.

From the world of mangas to the lands of tolkien, fantasy and psychologic themes are the main subjects that keep my attention. Furthermore, I'm also passionate about mythologies and tales that i think, are shaping societies.

I am convinced that telling stories is the way that humans choose to express themselves, teach, learn, and imagine other ways of seeing life (as well as other ways of living, and how to improve society).

Dystopia and science fiction are one of my favorite genres, because they aim to prevent society by showing it what not to do.

I'm sure that to find what we think we need, we must know what we don't need first, right ?
